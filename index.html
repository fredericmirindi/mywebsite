Academic Background

I have a background in Macroeconomics, Econometrics & Development Economics. My journey began at the Catholic University of Bukavu, where I earned my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Economics. I later pursued a M.Sc. in Development Evaluation at the University of Antwerp and completed Ph.D. coursework in Economics at Charles University, CERGE-EI.

Research Interests

My research interests include macroeconomic policy, econometric modeling, and economic development in Sub-Saharan Africa. I have received several awards and honors for my contributions to the field.
